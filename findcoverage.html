Reliable Resources

Finding reliable resources for healthcare information can be crucial in making informed decisions about your health. Let's review some of the trusted sources you can turn to for accurate and up-to-date healthcare information.

Healthcare.gov

First, we have healthcare.gov. This is a government website that provides information on health insurance options, including the Affordable Care Act (ACA) marketplace. You can find details about different plans, coverage options, and how to apply for health insurance through this platform. Healthcare.gov will also help you find out if you qualify for the Advanced Premium Tax Credit (APTC) which can help lower your monthly health insurance costs.

Visit healthcare.gov

Via Benefits Insurance Services

Next, we have my.viabenefits.com. Via Benefits Insurance Services is a trustworthy brokerage that offers information on various health insurance plans and benefits. They can help you navigate Health Insurance options on the Marketplace and Medicare options as well. They also offer a large selection of ancillary plans such as dental, vision, hearing, legal plans, foreign travel policies, and more. You can view their website or call them directly at 866-322-2824.

Visit my.viabenefits.com

Medicare.gov

For folks 65 and over or who are on Medicare due to disability, Medicare.gov is the go-to resource. This official government website provides comprehensive information about Medicare plans, coverage options, and how to enroll. You can also find tools to compare different Medicare plans and understand your benefits.

Visit Medicare.gov

Medicaid.gov

For individuals who qualify for Medicaid, Medicaid.gov is the official government website that provides information about eligibility requirements, coverage options, and how to apply. This resource is particularly important for those seeking low-cost or free healthcare options. You can also contact your state's Medicaid office for more specific information about the programs available in your area.

Visit Medicaid.gov
